USC Graphics

USC Computer Graphics researchers are conducting cutting-edge research in many areas of computer graphics. We are also cooperating with experts in visual effects and gaming industry in Southern California and beyond. More information is available at the faculty links below.

Prospective PhD students: If you are interested in working with any of the faculty members, please apply to the USC Computer Science Department, and indicate the faculty members in your application and essay.
